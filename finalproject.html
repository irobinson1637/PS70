Final Project Proposal

Project Title: Never-Ending Spinning Top

The goal of this project is to create a spinning top that utilizes an electric motor and sensor technology to maintain its spin indefinitely. The top will be able to detect when its spin is slowing down and automatically adjust the motor's speed to keep it spinning.

Technical Details

  • Spinning top will be constructed using 3D printing technology
  • Electric motor and sensor technology will be used to detect and maintain spin speed
  • A microcontroller, such as an Arduino, will be used to control the motor and sensor
  • A user interface, such as a smartphone app, will allow the user to adjust settings and monitor the top's spin

Challenges and Solutions

  • Ensuring the spinning top remains balanced while spinning indefinitely
  • Synchronizing the sensor and motor to accurately detect and maintain spin speed
  • Designing a user-friendly interface for controlling the top's spin
